Transactions

The Transaction Section helps users track their Income and Expenses separately, ensuring financial clarity. Income transactions (e.g., salary, bonuses) are shown in green, while expenses (e.g., rent, bills) appear in red for easy identification. Users can filter, search, and edit transactions, with real-time total calculations displayed. Additionally, graphical insights like charts and export options enhance financial management

Calendar

The Calendar Section helps users mark important financial dates, such as bill payments, salary credits, and budget deadlines. It provides reminders and notifications to ensure timely transactions and avoid missed payments. Users can customize events, set recurring reminders, and sync with external calendars for seamless financial planning.

Payment Methods

The Payment Section allows users to manage transactions through multiple methods, including UPI, Cash, Card, and Others. It ensures a seamless payment experience by tracking and categorizing expenses based on the selected method. Users can view payment history, set default payment modes, and manage linked accounts for efficient financial management.

Categories

The Category Section helps users organize transactions under Salary, Business, Investment, Loan, Rent, and Other. It provides a structured view of income and expenses, making financial tracking easier. Users can customize categories, analyze spending patterns, and generate reports for better budgeting.
